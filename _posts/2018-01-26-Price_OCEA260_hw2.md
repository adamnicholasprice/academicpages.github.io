#### Question 1b


```python
np.random.seed(0)
y = np.random.randn(1000)
sigma = np.std(y)
mean = np.mean(y)
gauss = (1/(sigma*(np.sqrt(2*np.pi)))*(np.exp(-((y-mean)**2)/(2*(sigma**2)))))
```


```python
plt.hist(y, bins = 30,normed = True)
plt.plot(y,gauss,'.')
plt.axis([-4,4,0,0.5])
plt.xlabel('Value')
plt.ylabel('Freq')
```
